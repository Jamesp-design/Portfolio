Experience

  1. Experience design within Moody’s Analytics, contributing to new product innovation for teams who need clearer tools around risk, compliance, and research workflows.

    • Green-field and incubation-style work for financial and compliance products — from discovery through delivery
    • Design for shell-company risk offerings and broader platform initiatives alongside engineering and product partners
    • Advancing design system coverage, quality, and patterns teams can reuse with confidence
    • Process and ways-of-working design to help teams ship faster without losing clarity
    • Internal design advocacy, critiques, and virtual conferences that raise the bar for craft

  2. Product design for a consumer Bitcoin app in a tight, UX-first team — shipping features fast while keeping trust and clarity at the centre of the experience.

    • End-to-end mobile features and flows across payments, onboarding, and core product surfaces
    • Full-app redesign initiative to align UI, patterns, and brand as the product matured
    • Clickable prototypes and proof-of-concepts for sales and partnerships
    • Collaboration with marketing on animated collateral and campaign assets
    • Close iteration with engineering on feasibility, edge cases, and polish

  3. Platform and integration design for institutional cryptocurrency solutions — making complex APIs and control surfaces understandable for enterprise buyers and operators.

    • Integration site and partner-facing tools for API exploration and technical storytelling
    • Internal admin and control surfaces for operations teams managing large-scale deployments
    • Sales-enablement decks, narratives, and materials for institutional audiences
    • Partnering with engineering on information architecture, states, and edge-case handling

  4. UI art for Assassin’s Creed Nexus VR (Meta Quest) — a full-length series entry built for wire-free VR, with HUD and diegetic work that stays readable across parkour, stealth, and combat.

    • HUD and in-headset UI supporting long play sessions alongside comfort, motion, and clarity constraints
    • Close collaboration with UX, design, and engineering on legibility, feedback, and VR-specific patterns
    • Iteration on hierarchy, iconography, and states so interfaces hold up during fast movement and combat

  5. Agency-side product design at Komodo Digital — shipping interfaces for client teams in fast discovery–delivery cycles across sectors.

    • End-to-end UX and UI for web and mobile products under tight client timelines
    • Workshops, prototyping, and clickable demos to align stakeholders before build
    • Handoff, QA, and iteration with developers to preserve design intent in production

  6. Product design for Hive HR’s employee engagement platform — features used at scale to help teams measure morale, act on feedback, and keep people connected.

    • Flows and components for surveys, insights, and manager tooling
    • Accessibility and clarity for diverse workforces and device contexts
    • Design system contributions as the product surface area grew

  7. UX and product design for cybersecurity tooling — making complex threat and configuration data scannable for analysts and IT teams.

    • Information architecture for dense, technical datasets and alerts
    • Patterns for status, severity, and drill-down without overwhelming operators
    • Collaboration with SMEs to validate language, workflows, and edge cases

  8. High-intensity build events and open-innovation formats — rapid prototyping, pitching, and cross-discipline collaboration with industry partners.

    • Hackathons with Atom Bank, Meta (Facebook), and other partners — from problem framing to demo day
    • Experience turning research and storyboards into clickable proofs overnight
    • Presentation and critique skills that still inform how I workshop with teams today
