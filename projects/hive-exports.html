Hive

Bringing a forgetton feature up to speed with the rest of the product.

  • UX Design
  • Design System
  • Accessibility

Feature Usage

At first, this feature really was not being used with only 36 interactions in total in the last 6 months. I needed to create something that encourages users to interact with it, while balancing cost vs value and keeping the scope MVP.

Wireframing and Iteration

The project was highly iterative. I developed quick wireframes with small changes, tested often, and used feedback to improve the solution step by step.

Final Modal Design

After refinement, the final export modal removed clutter, improved hierarchy, and aligned with the wider design system.
