Sync

An innovative new way to interact with music and the space around you.

  • VR Experience
  • Audio-visual

Brief

Create a sensor-based interface to let users interact with an audio-visual environment, using either physical computing or motion tracking.

Solution

I built a VR game where player position changed the soundscape. The goal was to find where distorted tracks synchronized and sounded normal, using Unity and HTC Vive.

Outcome

I expanded the concept with mapped playspace safety, additional object-based levels, and a complete in-game UI for a stand-alone product feel.
