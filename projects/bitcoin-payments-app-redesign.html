Bottlepay

A structural and visual overhaul to reflect the new direction of a consumer app.

  • UX Design
  • Mobile App
  • Design System

Brief

After two years of growth, feature sprawl made key actions harder to find and slowed future development.

Solution

We refreshed the app around user learnings and business direction, creating a robust structure flexible enough for future features.

Restructure

Through heavy iteration, we validated a 3-tab navigation model and reintroduced quick actions through a revised home experience.
